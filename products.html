VCBS-I

An innovation CBS product provides customized optimal brake force distribution ratio to have excellent brake performance.

VCBS-I Schematic

VCBS-I module

When the right handle is pressed, the right total mercury oil pressure independently controls the front wheel calipers.

HPVPressure Relief Valve

When pressing the left handle, the braking force of the front and rear wheels is distributed according to the pressing force of the handle

BFDPressure Relief Valve

When the left handle is pressed, the front wheel brake oil pressure is generated

HPVHydraulic Pressure Valve

When pressing the left handle, the braking force of the front and rear wheels is distributed according to the pressing force of the handle

BFDBrake force distribution

When the left handle is pressed, the front wheel brake oil pressure is generated

Features and advantages

1.Have high braking effort

2.Robustness : adaptable to the wear of rear brake shoes

3.Less changes to the original car configuration

4.Great flexibility in matching different types of vehicles

5.Do not kick each other with left and right hands

6.First left and then right don't fail

VCBS-I Specification

VCBS-I Specification

Size (LxWxH) 120 mm × 57 mm × 28 mm
Variation range of front and rear brake force ratio F/R：0/100~55/45 (Optimized according to different target vehicles)
Modular power efficiency Single power transmission> 80%
Estimated deceleration of L3 class vehicles Left handle input force ≥ 200N, Deceleration ≥ 5.9m/s2 (The maximum deceleration varies according to the specifications of the target car. The legal standard: deceleration ≥ 5.1m/s2)) (different depending on the target car)
The front wheel brake is disconnected, and the vehicle deceleration ≥ 3.1m/s2 (Regulatory standard: deceleration ≥ 2.9m/s2)) (depending on the target vehicle)
