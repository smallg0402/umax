Our History

  • 2014
    Into R&D

    Create an innovative R&D team that wants to break through the status quo, and invest in the first self-developed product – drum shoe wearing compensator (SWC)

  • 2015
    Supporting from the leading new product program of MOEA

    Proposed an innovative concept of CBS products, which was supported by the leading new product plan of MOEA in TAIWAN.

  • 2017
    Supportting from MOEA to encourage companies in Taiwan to set up R&D centers

    To improve the R&D system, the MOEA has encouraged enterprises in Taiwan to set up R&D centers, and integrate R&D, development, production and quality procedures.

  • 2021
    R&D products have been affirmed by motorcycle manufacturers

    *KYMCO GP125 VCS equipped with VCBS-II was launched
    *PGO ZAN V-CBS equipped with VCBS-I was released, which was widely praised.

  • 2022
    UMAX Technology was established

    A new R&D company, UMAX Technology, was established, shouldering the mission of innovation and opening up new horizons for innovation in the vehicle industry
